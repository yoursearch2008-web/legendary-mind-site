Common questions

Is this safe? Do you hold my tokens?
No. All tools are 100% client-side. Your Phantom wallet signs every transaction locally — nothing is sent to any server. You can inspect every transaction before signing.
How much SOL can I reclaim with SolSweep?
Each empty token account holds ~0.002 SOL as a rent deposit. A typical active DeFi wallet has 10–30 of these, so most users reclaim 0.02–0.10 SOL on their first sweep.
What are the fees?
SolSweep takes 10% of the reclaimed rent (e.g. you get 0.018 SOL back from a 0.020 SOL account). SolSwap takes 0.3% referral from Jupiter. All other tools charge only the Solana network gas fee.
Which wallet is supported?
Phantom wallet on desktop and mobile. More wallets coming soon.
