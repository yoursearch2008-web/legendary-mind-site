How it works: every SPL token account locks ~0.002 SOL as rent. When you no longer hold that token, the account sits empty but the SOL stays locked. SolSweep finds every empty account and closes them in one click — the rent comes back to you.

Fee: a 10% fee on recovered SOL is included in the same transaction you approve. No recovery, no fee. You always see the exact amounts before signing.

Safety: 100% in your browser. We only build a transaction; you approve it in your wallet. We never see your seed phrase or private key. Only empty (zero-balance) accounts are closed — your tokens are never touched.
