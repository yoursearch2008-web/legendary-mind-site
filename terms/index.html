2. What SolTools is

SolTools provides free, browser-based utilities for the Solana blockchain:

  • SolSweep — closes empty SPL token accounts and reclaims rent-locked SOL (10% fee of recovered amount)
  • SolSwap — token swaps via Jupiter aggregator (0.3% referral fee)
  • SolMint — SPL token creation (0.05 SOL flat fee)
  • SolSend — multi-recipient SOL/token transfers (0.001 SOL per recipient)
  • SolBurn — burn unwanted tokens and recover rent (0.001 SOL per account closed)
