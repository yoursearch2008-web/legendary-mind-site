Best GTA V Mods 2025

PC only but worth it. These mods make GTA V look and feel better than most 2025 releases. No cap this list is fire.

← Back to home

Essential First — Install These

🔧

Script Hook V + Native Trainer

Required for almost every mod. Install this first or nothing else works. Free at Alexander Blade's site. Update it whenever GTA V updates or your game will crash on launch.

🔧

OpenIV

The mod manager for GTA V. Lets you install, manage and remove mods safely. Also required by most graphics and vehicle mods. Free and essential fr fr.

Graphics Mods — Make It Look Next-Gen

🎨

NaturalVision Evolved

The GOAT of GTA V graphics mods. Completely rebuilds lighting, weather, textures and atmosphere. Makes GTA V look genuinely better than most 2025 releases. Some screenshots are literally indistinguishable from real life. The best graphics mod period.

🎨

VisualV

Free alternative to NaturalVision. Great lighting overhaul, improved weather, enhanced color grading. Easier to install and less performance-heavy. Solid if your PC can't handle NaturalVision.

🎨

Redux

All-in-one overhaul — graphics, weather, traffic, peds, police AI. One install that upgrades basically everything. Great starting point if you don't want to mix and match mods.

Gameplay Mods — Change How It Plays

⚙️

Realistic Driving V

Replaces the arcade handling with simulation physics. Cars actually feel heavy, tires grip realistically, crashes matter. If you're used to GTA V's floaty driving this will hit different — in a good way.

⚙️

Menyoo PC

The best trainer/sandbox mod. Spawn anything, change weather, freeze wanted level, teleport, edit your character. Essential for messing around and content creation. Free.

⚙️

LSPD First Response (LSPDFR)

Play as a cop. Pull over traffic, respond to crimes, chase suspects. Completely changes the game loop. Massive modding community on top of it. If you've seen GTA V cop roleplay on YouTube, this is what they're using.

Map & World Mods

🗺️

FiveM

Custom multiplayer servers. Roleplay servers, racing servers, death match servers — thousands of custom servers with custom maps and rules. This is where GTA V RP content comes from. Free to download, connects to community servers.

🗺️

GTA V Liberty City

The entire GTA IV map ported into GTA V. Drive, fly, and explore Liberty City in GTA V's engine. Absolutely wild as a crossover experience and one of the most ambitious fan projects in gaming history.

⚠️

Mods = Story Mode only

Never use mods in GTA Online — Rockstar will ban your account. Keep a separate GTA V installation for mods if you want to play Online too. OpenIV has a built-in "mods" folder system to keep them separate.

The stack 💀

Install in this order: Script Hook V → OpenIV → NaturalVision Evolved → Realistic Driving V → Menyoo. That five-mod stack makes GTA V feel like a completely different (better) game. You will not go back to vanilla after this fr fr.
