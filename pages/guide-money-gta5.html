Guide 02

Make Money Fast in GTA V

Best money methods still working in 2026. No glitches, no Shark Cards — just W plays.

← Back to home

Tier 1 — Solo Grind (New Players)

💰

VIP / CEO Work

$30,000–$50,000 per job. Register as VIP (need $50k), run Headhunter and Sightseer jobs. Fast, solo, reliable. Best starting grind in 2026.

🏍️

MC Businesses (Passive)

Set up cocaine lockup or meth lab, resupply regularly, sell in full lobbies for 2x money during bonus weeks. Set and forget money printer.

🔫

Bunker

Paleto Bay bunker is cheap. Stock it, let it run, sell $210k worth when full. One of the best passive businesses in the game.

Tier 2 — Mid Game

🚗

Import/Export

$80,000–$100,000 per top-range vehicle. Buy CEO office + vehicle warehouse. Source and export top-range cars only. Skip standard and mid-range entirely.

🏗️

Nightclub

Links all your businesses and generates passive income. Takes time to set up but prints money once it's running. Don't neglect the safe.

Tier 3 — Endgame

🎯

The Cayo Perico Heist (Solo)

Up to $1.9M per run. Solo heist. Preps take ~45 mins, finale ~15 mins. Best money per hour in the game if you're efficient. Run it on repeat during 2x money events.

👥

The Diamond Casino Heist (Group)

$2.1M–$3.6M with a good crew. Needs 2–4 players. Best payout in the game with the right team. Silent & Sneaky approach for max cash.

Bonus Week Strategy

Rockstar runs 2x/3x money events every week. Check what's boosted before grinding — doing the wrong thing at 1x when you could wait for 2x is leaving money on the table. Check the Rockstar Newswire every Thursday.

Best route in 2026 💀

VIP work → Bunker → Import/Export → Cayo Perico on repeat. Stack $5M, buy a Nightclub, then you're printing passive money while running heists. We are not okay with how much the Cayo pays fr fr.
