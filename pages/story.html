GTA V's Story

Three protagonists, one of the most iconic gaming stories ever told. Michael is the burned-out retired criminal. Franklin is the hungry up-and-comer. Trevor is absolute chaos incarnate. The way their stories intersect is genuinely cinematic — GTA V's campaign is still one of the best in gaming, no cap.

Around 30 hours of main story. Heists are peak. The ending (whichever you pick) hits different.

GTA VI's Story

Lucia and Jason — a Bonnie and Clyde-inspired duo. GTA VI is the first mainline game with a female protagonist. The story is set in a modern Florida-inspired world with social media, influencers, and real commentary on American culture. Early reports say it's more focused and emotional than GTA V's sprawling three-way story.
